### Risk Assessment Methodology

## Purpose
To identify, assess, mitigate, and monitor risks that could
impact the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of
TujaePays systems and customer data.

## Scope
Applies to:
- Information security
- Technology operations
- Regulatory compliance
- Third-party vendors
- Business continuity

## Risk Identification
Risks are identified through:
- Internal audits
- Vendor assessments
- Incident reviews
- Regulatory changes
- Employee reporting

## Risk Scoring Model

### Likelihood (1–5)
| Score | Description |
|------|------------|
| 1 | Rare |
| 3 | Possible |
| 5 | Almost Certain |

### Impact (1–5)
| Score | Description |
|------|------------|
| 1 | Minimal |
| 3 | Moderate |
| 5 | Severe (financial, regulatory, reputational) |

### Risk Rating Formula
Risk Score= Likelihood x Impact

### Risk Levels
| Score | Level |
|------|------|
| 1–6 | Low |
| 7–14 | Medium |
| 15–25 | High |

## Risk Treatment Options
- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept
- Avoid

## Review Frequency
- High Risk: Quarterly
- Medium Risk: Semi-Annually
- Low Risk: Annually
