### Access Control Policy


**1. Purpose**

This policy defines requirements for controlling access to TujaePay information systems to ensure only authorized individuals have appropriate access.

**2. Scope**
   
This policy applies to all users, systems, applications, and data owned or managed by TujaePay.

**3. Access Principles**

TujaePay follows the principles of:

  - Least privilege
  - Role-based access control (RBAC)
  - Separation of duties

**4. User Access Management**

-  All access must be approved by management
-  Access is granted based on job responsibilities
-  Privileged access requires additional approval

**5. Authentication Requirements**

-  Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all production systems
-  Strong password standards must be followed
-  Shared accounts are prohibited

**6. Access Reviews**

-  User access is reviewed at least quarterly
-  Access is revoked promptly upon termination or role change

**7. Logging and Monitoring**

Access to sensitive systems is logged and monitored for unauthorized or suspicious activity.

**8. Policy Enforcement**

Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action and access revocation.
