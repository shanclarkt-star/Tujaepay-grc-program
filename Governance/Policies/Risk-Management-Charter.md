### Risk Management Charter


**1. Purpose**

The purpose of this charter is to define TujaePay’s enterprise risk management (ERM) framework and governance structure.

**2. Scope**

This charter applies to all business units, systems, processes, and third-party relationships.

**3. Risk Management Objectives**

TujaePay aims to:

-  Identify and assess risks proactively
-  Reduce risks to acceptable levels
-  Support informed decision-making
-  Protect customers and stakeholders

**4. Risk Categories**

Risks are categorized as:

-  Information Security
-  Compliance and Regulatory
-  Operational
-  Financial
-  Third-Party
   
**5. Risk Assessment**

Risks are assessed based on:

-  Likelihood
-  Impact
-  Inherent Risk
-  Residual Risk

**6. Risk Ownership**

Each risk is assigned an owner responsible for mitigation and monitoring.

**7. Risk Acceptance**

Risks exceeding TujaePay’s risk tolerance must be escalated to executive management.

**8. Reporting**

Risk metrics and high-risk issues are reported quarterly to leadership.

**9. Review**

This charter is reviewed annually or upon significant organizational changes.
