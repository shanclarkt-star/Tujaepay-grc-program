### Incident Response Policy

**1. Purpose**

This policy establishes a structured approach for responding to information security incidents to minimize impact and restore operations.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all security incidents involving AtlasPay systems, data, or services.

**3. Incident Definition**

A security incident includes:

-  Unauthorized access or disclosure
-  Malware or ransomware infections
-  Data loss or system compromise
-  Denial of service attacks

**4. Incident Response Phases**

AtlasPay follows these phases:

-  Identification
-  Containment
-  Eradication
-  Recovery
- Lessons Learned  

**5. Roles and Responsibilities**

-  **Incident Response Team:** Manages and coordinates response
-  **Employees:** Report suspected incidents immediately
-  **Management:** Provides decision-making and communication support

**6. Reporting**

All incidents must be reported to the GRC or Security Team without delay.

**7. Communication**

Incident communications are restricted to authorized personnel to prevent misinformation.

**8. Testing and Training**
   
Incident response procedures are tested annually through tabletop exercises.

**9. Review**
    
This policy is reviewed annually or following a significant incident.
