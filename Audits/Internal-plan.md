# Internal Audit Plan

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Internal Audit Plan is to define TujaePay’s approach to evaluating the
effectiveness of governance, risk management, and internal controls. The plan supports
management assurance, regulatory expectations, and continuous improvement.

---

## 2. Audit Objectives
Internal audits are designed to:
- Assess the effectiveness of internal controls
- Identify control gaps and risk exposures
- Validate compliance with internal policies and external frameworks
- Provide actionable recommendations to management

---

## 3. Scope
The internal audit program covers the following areas:

- Information Security Governance
- Access Control 
- Risk Management Processes
- Third-Party Risk Management
- Incident Response Preparedness
- Compliance with NIST CSF and SOC 2 principles

Audit scope may be adjusted based on emerging risks or business changes.

---

## 4. Audit Frequency
- High-risk areas: Annually
- Medium-risk areas: Every 18–24 months
- Low-risk areas: As needed

---

## 5. Audit Methodology
Audits are conducted using a risk-based approach and include:
- Policy and procedure review
- Interviews with control owners
- Evidence inspection (logs, configurations, reports)
- Control design and operating effectiveness testing

---

## 6. Roles and Responsibilities

- Internal Audit / GRC Team:
  - Plan and execute audits
  - Document findings and recommendations
- Control Owners:
  - Provide requested evidence
  - Support remediation activities
- Executive Management:
  - Review audit results
  - Approve remediation plans

---

## 7. Reporting
Audit results are documented in a formal Audit Findings Report and communicated to
management. Findings are tracked to closure.

---

## 8. Issue Tracking and Remediation
Audit findings are:
- Risk-rated (Low / Medium / High)
- Assigned an owner
- Tracked until remediation is completed

Unresolved high-risk issues are escalated to leadership.

---

## 9. Review and Maintenance
This Internal Audit Plan is reviewed annually and updated as necessary.
