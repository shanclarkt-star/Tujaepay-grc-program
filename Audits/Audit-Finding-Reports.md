# Audit Findings Report

## Audit Overview

- Audit Name: GRC and Security Controls Review
- Audit Period: Q4 2025
- Audit Performed By: GRC / Internal Audit Team
- Audit Scope:
  - Access Control
  - Risk Management
  - Third-Party Risk Management
  - Incident Response

---

## Audit Summary
The audit identified that TujaePay has a generally effective control environment.
Most key controls are implemented and operating as designed. Opportunities for
improvement were identified in documentation consistency and formalization of
review processes.

---

## Audit Findings Summary

| Finding ID | Area | Risk Rating | Status |
|-----------|------|-------------|--------|
| F-001 | Access Reviews | Medium | Open |
| F-002 | Vendor Risk Documentation | Low | Open |
| F-003 | Incident Response Testing | Medium | Open |

---

## Detailed Findings

### Finding ID: F-001
- Control Area: Access Control
- Risk Rating: Medium

**Observation**  
User access reviews are performed; however, evidence of quarterly reviews is not
consistently documented.

**Risk**  
Lack of documented access reviews increases the risk of unauthorized or excessive
access remaining undetected.

**Recommendation**  
Formalize quarterly access review documentation and retain evidence for audit purposes.

**Management Response**  
Management agrees and will implement standardized access review templates.

**Target Remediation Date**  
March 2026

---

### Finding ID: F-002
- Control Area: Third-Party Risk Management
- Risk Rating: Low

**Observation**  
Vendor risk assessments are performed but not consistently stored in a centralized
repository.

**Risk**  
Inconsistent documentation may impact audit readiness.

**Recommendation**  
Centralize vendor risk assessments within the GRC repository.

**Management Response**  
Management agrees and will update documentation practices.

**Target Remediation Date**  
February 2026

---

### Finding ID: F-003
- Control Area: Incident Response
- Risk Rating: Medium

**Observation**  
Incident response plans exist, but tabletop exercises have not been conducted within
the last 12 months.

**Risk**  
Lack of testing may delay effective response during a real incident.

**Recommendation**  
Conduct annual incident response tabletop exercises and document results.

**Management Response**  
Management agrees and will schedule tabletop exercises.

**Target Remediation Date**  
April 2026

---

## Conclusion
The audit confirms that TujaePay has established foundational GRC and security controls.
Addressing the identified findings will further strengthen the control environment and
support regulatory and audit readiness.

---

## Follow-Up
Audit findings will be re-evaluated during the next audit cycle to confirm remediation.
